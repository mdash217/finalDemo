Serving Our Community Since '91

Family Owned and Operated

Started in 1991 by a mother and son team, El Pulgarcito arose out of a need for afforable homestyle food in the low income neighborhood of Cypress Hills. 30 years later we pride ourselves on still being family owned and operated and serving traditional, authentic Salvadoran food just like grandma made.
